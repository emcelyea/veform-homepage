Let your users speak

Dead simple iOS, Web and Android library for turning traditional inputs into voice conversations.

Documentation

Why?

  • The future is users having the option to speak with their apps.
  • Interacting with an app should be like talking to a friend or coworker.
  • Repeatedly tapping input into a screen is a chore.
  • Apps should offer the level of accessibility that conversations provide.

Seriously customizable

UI/UX is yours, you shouldn't compromise your vision an inch for the sake of a library.

  • Want a conversation only and no traditional UI?
  • Want users to be able to touch/type into inputs while having a conversation?
  • Want one touch input and the rest to be conversational?
  • We support all that. Our philosophy is Veform offloads a specific and challenging subset of problems, but has no opinion about how you use it.
